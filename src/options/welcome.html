Welcome to RollCloud!

DiceCloud + Roll20 + Discord Integration

Getting Started

1

Sync Your Character

Open dicecloud.com, go to your character sheet, then click the RollCloud icon in your toolbar and click "Refresh Characters"

2

Open Roll20

Go to your Roll20 game. Click "Show Character Sheet" in the popup to see your character overlay

3

Connect Discord (Optional)

In the popup, expand "Discord Integration" and click "Setup Discord". You'll get a 6-character code to type in your Discord channel with /rollcloud CODE

Tip: Click the RollCloud icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup anytime!
