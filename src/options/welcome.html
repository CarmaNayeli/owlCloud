Welcome to OwlCloud!

DiceCloud + Owlbear Rodeo + Discord Integration

Getting Started

1

Sync Your Character

Open dicecloud.com, go to your character sheet, then click the OwlCloud icon in your toolbar and click "Refresh Characters"

2

Open Owlbear Rodeo

Go to your Owlbear Rodeo game. The extension will integrate with Owlbear when active

3

Connect Discord (Optional)

In the popup, expand "Discord Integration" and click "Setup Discord". You'll get a 6-character code to type in your Discord channel with /owlcloud CODE

Tip: Click the OwlCloud icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup anytime!
